Current Exhibition

Kinship

Angela Huizer · Photography

Kinship questions identity beyond bloodlines or geography. Born in the Netherlands and shaped by Javanese-Surinamese heritage, rooted in forced migration, Angela explores culture as lived and shared — allowing multiple answers to exist without resolution.

Through photography, archival materials, cyanotypes, and an installation, the work traces family histories, cultural rituals, and the ways memory and belonging move across generations and continents.

For this exhibition, Sauvage transforms into an intimate environment — a place where visitors can slow down and gather. The space will feel a bit like a living room, so we'll invite everyone to take off their shoes when entering.

Curated by Wisse Kodde
